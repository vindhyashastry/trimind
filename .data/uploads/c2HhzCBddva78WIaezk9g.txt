6.1 Either party may terminate this Agreement with 60 days written notice.
6.2 In case of material breach, the non-breaching party may terminate immediately upon written notice.
6.3 Upon termination, Provider shall return all Client materials and deliver any work in progress.

7.1 Provider's total liability shall not exceed the total fees paid under this Agreement.
7.2 Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.

SECTION 8: COMPLIANCE AND REGULATORY
8.1 Provider shall comply with all applicable laws, regulations, and industry standards.
8.2 Provider must maintain SOC 2 Type II certification throughout the engagement.
8.3 Regular compliance audits may be conducted by Client or its designated auditors.
